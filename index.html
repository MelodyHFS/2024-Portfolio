About Me

My name is Melody Simpson and I'm a student at Cab Calloway School of the Arts. I'm a Communication Arts major that works with typography, photography, and other mixed digital media. Some of the programs I know how to use are Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, HTML, and CSS. I've helped film livestreams at Cab Calloway and worked as stage crew on different plays.
